## What It Does

Answer ~20 questions about your AI system and get:
- **Risk classification** (Low / Medium / High)
- **Applicable regulations** mapped to your system
- **Sector-specific obligations** (if Education, Health, Finance, or Government)
- **Compliance checklist** with required vs recommended actions
